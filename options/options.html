Getting started

Here are some tips to help you get going with list.it:

  • Open and close list.it by clicking on the icon in the upper right of your screen.
  • Make new notes by typing into the box at the top of list.it's sidebar.
  • Search your notes by typing into the (search bar) in the sidebar.
  • Delete notes by clicking on .
  • Edit notes by clicking one and typing, when you select something else, the note will automatically save.
  • Coming soon: Note reordering + Saved Searches!
